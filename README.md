# Construction Analytics Solution

> An end-to-end business intelligence and analytics solution that demonstrates how real-world construction operations can be transformed into actionable business insights through solution architecture, database design, SQL analytics, and interactive dashboards.

---

# Overview

The Construction Analytics Solution is a professional portfolio project based on over 30 years of commercial construction experience spanning field operations, project management, estimating, purchasing, executive administration, payroll, and Vice President of Job Cost.

The project recreates the design of a construction performance management system that provides near real-time visibility into project performance. Rather than focusing solely on financial reporting after projects are complete, the solution is designed to help management identify issues while work is still in progress, allowing corrective action before costs are finalized.

The project demonstrates the complete lifecycle of an analytics solution—from business discovery and solution architecture through database design, SQL development, and business intelligence dashboards.

---

# Business Problem

Commercial construction companies generate operational and financial data from multiple systems, including:

- Estimating
- Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
- Project Management
- Field Time Entry
- Equipment Tracking
- Material Usage
- Purchasing
- Payroll
- Accounting

Although this information exists, meaningful project performance reporting is often unavailable until payroll, equipment, material, and accounting processes have been completed.

As a result, managers frequently ask questions such as:

- How is the job performing today?
- Are we exceeding our labor budget?
- Which cost codes are creating the largest variances?
- Are production rates meeting expectations?
- Are we still on track to achieve our target margin?
- Which projects require immediate management attention?

The Construction Analytics Solution is designed to answer those questions using operational information before traditional financial reporting is complete.

# Project Objectives

The primary objectives of this project are to:

- Design a normalized MySQL relational database
- Develop a reusable SQL analytics layer
- Centralize business logic and KPI calculations within SQL
- Create interactive dashboards in Power BI
- Recreate the solution using Tableau
- Demonstrate professional software engineering and documentation practices
- Showcase complete solution architecture from business discovery through implementation

# Why This Project?

Most business intelligence portfolio projects begin with a database and end with a dashboard.

This project takes a different approach.

The Construction Analytics Solution is designed using a business-first methodology that begins with understanding how experienced construction professionals make decisions. The solution architecture, database, SQL analytics layer, and dashboards are all driven by documented business requirements rather than technology alone.

The project combines decades of commercial construction experience with formal education in management, information systems, accounting, and finance to demonstrate how operational knowledge can be transformed into a modern analytics platform.

The goal is to showcase not only technical skills, but also the ability to bridge business operations and technology through thoughtful solution design.
