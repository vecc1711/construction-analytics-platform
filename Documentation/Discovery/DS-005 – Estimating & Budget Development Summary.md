DS-005 – Estimating & Budget Development Summary
Purpose
    This workshop examined how project estimates are developed, converted into operational budgets, and continuously improved through historical project performance.

Key Business Discoveries
Customer Relationships
    Repeat customers represent the majority of annual work and influence pricing strategy.

Operational Planning
    Estimating establishes the execution plan rather than simply determining project price.

Production Standards
    Production standards combine historical performance with estimator experience and project-specific conditions.

Standard Crews
    Standard crew configurations improve estimating consistency while allowing project-specific adjustments.

Budget Development
    The approved job budget represents a reviewed and normalized version of the original estimate.

Continuous Improvement
    Closed Job Reviews improve future estimating accuracy through structured organizational learning.

Business Impact
    Estimating provides the baseline against which all operational and financial performance is measured.
